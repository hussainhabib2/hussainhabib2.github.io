It's All About Your Dukan

Grocery store customers all over the world are concerned about their health safety but also need groceries. The shopping experience of the grocery stores had been focused on the convenience and appeal of the visit to the store and the speed of checkout. This has changed very quickly. Many of the grocery stores around the world were not ready for a sea change. We bridge the gap by providing means for grocery stores to continue serving their loyal customers. Consumers can now safely order groceries from your grocery store from their mobile phones. We securely convey the orders to you and help you manage their orders in an efficient manner from start to finish. We can get your grocery store up and running online with a supporting iOS and Android app within 24 hours.

Business Features

01.

Data Security

Your transactions between the website and the mobile or web app are secured by a TLS 2048-bit certificate. This high security ensures the privacy of your customer’s data and your data. It is an essential consideration for cybersecurity.

02.

Smartphone

Manage your orders right from your smartphone – not you on the phone. By taking orders electronically, your staff does not have to take orders manually and does not have to guess the size, quantity, or color of the order.

03.

Stay in Touch

Do not be afraid of missing a customer call or having to return calls. They can order at any time and you can rest easy knowing that the system will keep their order in a queue for you to attend to it.

04.

Availability

Give your customers the freedom to order whenever they want, without causing interruption to you or your staff. Your staff stays focused on the processes and procedures that you have for your business while your customers use their smartphones and web to place their orders conveniently.

05.

POS System

We offer a backup POS System that can scan right from your mobile phone and charge the customer’s charge card. This can help you quickly go through long customer lines during peak times. While the cashiers at the counters may be busy with customers, an employee can take his or her smartphone and checkout the next person waiting in line.

06.

Reporting

Grab the opportunities present in the trends by looking at the analytical reports that we provide by sifting through your sales data. This can help you make important decisions such as which brands and products to promote and what price to set. These reports include reports on customers, products, orders, and sales.

07.

Bulk Manage

Manage your inventory in Excel and then upload it to the system for immediate availability with updates. Likewise, you can download your products anytime and use Microsoft Excel to manage them, including sections, prices, barcodes, etc.

08.

Customization

Choose if you want to allow pickups, delivery, or both and what the fee is going to be for the service. Tailor your privacy policy or terms and conditions of service. Your online store is a reflection of your business.

09.

Marketing Campaigns

Send notifications with pictures of your promoted products to the smartphones of your customers with ease. Send any sales or specials messages. This gets your message right in the palms of your customers.

10.

Visualize Products

Add products to your system and attach pictures of them right from your smartphone. This makes sure your customer makes an informed decision when selecting the product to purchase. You can attach as many pictures as you like for each product.

11.

Visualize Store

Add 360 degrees panoramic images of your store for your customers to get familiar with your store. You can easily add images of the exterior as well as the interior of your store. Your customers can see what is available in your store through the interior 360 degrees images.

12.

Payment Processing

Payments are processed promptly and securely using Stripe. When the customer places the order, a hold is put on the customer’s charge card, and when the order is ready to leave the store, the adjusted charge is made to the card presented by your customer.

13.

Easy to Use

You don’t need a technology expert to manage your online presence – there are no additional human resources expenses.

14.

Unlimited Accounts

There is no limit to the number of customer or staff accounts that you can create and manage. You may assign the roles of administrator, manager, cashier, the person you put together the order, or the delivery person.

Our Mission

You have spent years building a loyal customer base – keep them! The shopping experience has changed, the business model has not. Your customers still want the products that you sell, they still love the customer service that you diligently provide. The human aspects of the business remain essentially the same. Only the significance of technology has become more evident. You focus on the business that you have developed – we’ve you covered in technology.

Technology Features

Mobile Computing

We believe in the power of mobile computing and we want to give that power to your customers. Many of us spend hours everyday using cellphones. Having your store app on their phones makes it so much more convenient for them to order from you. Our mobile application is available in both iOS and Android, ready to be customized for your store. You can request a custom mobile app for your store only.

Responsive Web App

For the customers not wanting to install a mobile app on their smartphones, all your products are available on the web app as well. You may use a mobile phone, a tablet, or a desktop browser to browse, purchase, place order, or perform administrative tasks using the web app. The same web app works on mobile as well as desktop devices.

Easy to Use

All you need to get started is an Excel spreadsheet of your products. It can be readily uploaded and your items will be available instantly! We have a pool of images for various products that you can use for your products. You do not need to take pictures of all of your products. Of course, you have the option to change the images or add images, to any of your products using your cellphone or tablet. A simple barcode scan can get you going with new products.

Easy to Manage

All orders are automatically charged and easily managed from the time they are placed to delivery. You can assign orders to staff to fulfill, external contractors, to fulfill, and drivers to deliver. Your customer can use the app to ensure all items are delivered and have ready access to the receipt. Previous orders are available to the customer to reorder at a later date

Dedicated Servers

Your data resides on dedicated servers just for your store. It does not mix up with the data of other stores. In other words it is a single-tenancy model. This provides greater security and creates a more expandable and customizable environment. The entire environment can be transferred to a server of your choice upon request.

Scan to Order

The built-in scanner provides the ability to the customer to place items in their cart by simply using their cellphone to scan the barcode of the item. This makes it easy for them to reorder items. The items stay in their cart until they check out. When they receive their order, they can also check the delivery by scanning the items delivered.

Delivery Matches Order

Your staff member who puts the order together to be delivered or picked up sees the items in the list sorted by aisle and shelf. This allows him or her to make a single dash through the aisles only one time to pick up all the items. There is a scan facility available to make sure that the item put in cart matches the item ordered by the customer.

Security

All transactions are conducted using Transport Layer Security (TLS). Sensitive charge card information is never stored on your server. Thus there is no liability of someone stealing that information from your server. We take your and your customers' data security very seriously.

Ride on Trends

With graphical reports, you can easily spot shopping trends. The reports are available in PDF, Word, and Excel formats. This makes them easy to work with and print out for later reference. Good analytics is a key to a successful business today. It allows you to carefully set your strategies such as pricing, delivery, high-profit brands, and products, etc.
